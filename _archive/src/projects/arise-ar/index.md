## Overview

ARISE is an augmented reality scavenger hunt designed for pediatric hospital patients, encouraging them to get up, walk around, and engage with their environment through an immersive undersea adventure.

## Role

UX Designer and Engineer at SpellBound AR. Responsible for interaction design, AR experience design, and front-end implementation.

## Details

Hospital stays are stressful for children. ARISE reframes the hospital environment as an adventure — patients discover AR sea creatures and collect items hidden throughout the ward, making movement therapeutic and engaging rather than clinical.

Designed in close collaboration with hospital staff and child-life specialists to ensure the experience was clinically appropriate and genuinely delightful.
