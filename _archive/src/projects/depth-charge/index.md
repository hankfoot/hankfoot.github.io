## Overview

A fully self-contained handheld arcade cabinet modeled after the radar console of a WWII submarine. Players hunt enemy ships using sonar pings and drop depth charges to sink them.

## Details

Built entirely from scratch — custom PCB, hand-soldered electronics, laser-cut enclosure, and original firmware written in C. The game runs on a small OLED display with tactile arcade buttons.

The form factor draws from mid-century instrument panels — dense dials, rounded corners, warning labels. The goal was to make something that felt like an authentic artifact, not a toy.

Every component was chosen for tactility: the buttons have satisfying clicks, the speaker is loud, the enclosure has real heft.
