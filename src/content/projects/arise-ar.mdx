<Section label="summary">
ARISE is an augmented reality scavenger hunt for pediatric hospital patients, built to make the ward feel less clinical and more like an adventure. Kids discover AR sea creatures hidden throughout the hospital, collect items, and complete challenges — all while getting the movement their recovery requires.

The project was a UI refresh ahead of the app's beta launch, focused on the login flow, inventory system, and onboarding experience.
</Section>
