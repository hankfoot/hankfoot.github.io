<Section label="summary">
Quick Distract Cards are a deck of illustrated activity cards for pediatric patients facing anxiety-inducing procedures. Each card gives a child life specialist a fast, no-setup tool: a breathing exercise, a sensory game, a visualization prompt. The design had to work in a busy clinical environment — legible at a glance, durable, and inviting enough that kids actually want to engage.

The project extended SpellBound AR's product line beyond the screen, creating a tactile companion to their digital distraction tools.
</Section>
