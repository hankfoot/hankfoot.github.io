## Overview

An immersive arcade heist experience where players physically wield a stethoscope to listen for the tumblers inside a vault and crack it open before time runs out.

## Recognition

Winner of the ACM CHI Student Games Competition, 2020. Published as a full paper at ACM CHI 2020.

## Details

Designed and built as a graduate thesis project at Georgia Tech's College of Design. Explores how audio-first controllers and physical object interaction can deepen immersion in game experiences.

Key design questions: How does eliminating visual feedback change player behavior? How can familiar everyday objects become compelling game controllers?
